Electric Potential

Equation:

$$V = k \frac{q}{r}$$

Variables:

  • \(V\): Electric potential [V]
  • \(k\): Coulomb's constant [N·m²/C²]
  • \(q\): Point charge [C]
  • \(r\): Distance from the charge [m]
This equation calculates the electric potential at a distance \( r \) from a point charge \( q \). The electric potential is a scalar quantity and is measured in volts.
