Electric Field (\( \vec{E} \)) and Electric Potential (V)

Equation:

$$\vec{E} = -\nabla V$$

Variables:

  • \(\vec{E}\): Electric field vector [N/C]
  • \(V\): Electric potential [V]
  • \(\nabla V\): Gradient of the electric potential [V/m]
This equation relates the electric field to the gradient of the electric potential. It indicates that the electric field is directed in the direction of the greatest rate of decrease of the potential.
