Electrostatic Force

Equation:

$$\vec{F}_E = q\vec{E}$$

Variables:

  • \(\vec{F}_E\): Electrostatic force [N]
  • \(q\): Electric charge [C]
  • \(\vec{E}\): Electric field strength [N/C]
This equation describes the force exerted on a charge \(q\) in an electric field \(\vec{E}\). The force is proportional to the charge and the electric field strength.
