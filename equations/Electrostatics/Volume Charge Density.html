Charge Density (\(\rho\))

Equation:

$$\rho = \frac{Q}{V}$$

Variables:

  • \(\rho\): Charge density [C/m³]
  • \(Q\): Total charge [C]
  • \(V\): Volume [m³]
This equation represents the charge density, which is the amount of electric charge per unit volume. It is a fundamental concept in electromagnetism, describing how charge is distributed in a given space.
