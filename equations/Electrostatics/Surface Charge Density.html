Surface Charge Density (\(\sigma\))

Equation:

$$\sigma = \frac{Q}{A}$$

Variables:

  • \(\sigma\): Surface charge density [C/m²]
  • \(Q\): Total charge [C]
  • \(A\): Area [m²]
This equation relates the surface charge density to the total charge distributed over a specified area. It is fundamental in the study of electric fields and charge distributions.
