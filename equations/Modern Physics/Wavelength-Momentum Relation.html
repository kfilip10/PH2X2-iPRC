De Broglie Wavelength

Equation:

$$\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$$

Variables:

  • \(\lambda\): Wavelength [m]
  • \(h\): Planck's constant [J·s]
  • \(p\): Momentum of the particle [kg·m/s]
This equation defines the wavelength associated with a particle and is a fundamental concept in wave-particle duality. It relates the momentum of a particle to its wavelength.
