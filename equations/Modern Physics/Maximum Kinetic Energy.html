Maximum Kinetic Energy of Photoelectrons

Equation:

$$K_\text{max} = hf - \Phi$$

Variables:

This equation describes the photoelectric effect, relating the maximum kinetic energy of electrons ejected from a material to the energy of incident photons and the material's work function.
