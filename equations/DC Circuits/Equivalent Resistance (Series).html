Equivalent Resistance in Series

Equation:

$$R_\text{eq} = \sum_{j=1}^n R_j$$

Variables:

  • \(R_\text{eq}\): Equivalent resistance [Ω]
  • \(R_j\): Resistance of the j-th resistor [Ω]
This equation calculates the equivalent resistance of resistors connected in series. The total resistance is the sum of all individual resistances in the series.
