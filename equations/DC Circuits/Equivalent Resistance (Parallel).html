Equivalent Resistance in Parallel Circuits

Equation:

$$\frac{1}{R_\text{eq}} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{1}{R_j}$$

Variables:

  • \(R_\text{eq}\): Equivalent resistance of the parallel circuit [Ω]
  • \(R_j\): Resistance of the j-th resistor in the parallel circuit [Ω]
  • \(n\): Total number of resistors in the parallel circuit
This equation calculates the equivalent resistance of a parallel circuit, where each resistor contributes to a decrease in total resistance. The total conductance is the sum of the individual conductances.
