Capacitance (C)

Equation:

$$C = \frac{Q}{V}$$

Variables:

  • \(C\): Capacitance [F] (Farads)
  • \(Q\): Charge [C] (Coulombs)
  • \(V\): Voltage [V] (Volts)
This equation relates the capacitance of a capacitor to the charge it holds and the voltage across it. It defines the ability of a capacitor to store electrical energy.
