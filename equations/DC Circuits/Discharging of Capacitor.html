Charge Decay Over Time in an RC Circuit

Equation:

$$q(t) = q_0 e^{-t / \tau_c}$$

Variables:

  • \(q(t)\): Charge at time \(t\) [Coulombs]
  • \(q_0\): Initial charge [Coulombs]
  • \(e\): Base of the natural logarithm
  • \(t\): Time [s]
  • \(\tau_c\): Time constant of the circuit [s]
This equation describes the exponential decay of charge in an RC circuit over time. The time constant \(\tau_c\) determines how quickly the charge decreases.
