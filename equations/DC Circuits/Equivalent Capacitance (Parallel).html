Equivalent Capacitance for Capacitors in Parallel

Equation:

$$C_\text{eq} = \sum_{j=1}^n C_j$$

Variables:

  • \(C_\text{eq}\): Equivalent capacitance [F]
  • \(C_j\): Capacitance of the j-th capacitor in the parallel circuit [F]
  • \(n\): Number of capacitors
This equation gives the equivalent capacitance of capacitors arranged in parallel. The total capacitance is the sum of individual capacitances.
