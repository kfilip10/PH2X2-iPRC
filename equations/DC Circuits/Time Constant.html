Time Constant (\(\tau_c\))

Equation:

$$\tau_c = RC$$

Variables:

  • \(\tau_c\): Time constant [s]
  • R: Resistance [Î©]
  • C: Capacitance [F]
This equation describes the time constant of an RC circuit, which is the time required for the voltage across the capacitor to reach approximately 63.2% of its final value during charging or discharging.
