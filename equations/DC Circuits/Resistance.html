Resistance (R)

Equation:

$$R = \rho \frac{L}{A}$$

Variables:

  • \(R\): Resistance [Ω]
  • \(\rho\): Resistivity of the material [Ω·m]
  • \(L\): Length of the conductor [m]
  • \(A\): Cross-sectional area of the conductor [m²]
This equation calculates the resistance of a conductor using its physical properties, specifically the material's resistivity, its length, and cross-sectional area.
