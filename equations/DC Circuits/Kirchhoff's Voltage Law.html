Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)

Equation:

$$\sum V = 0$$

Variables:

  • \(\sum V\): The algebraic sum of all voltages in a closed loop or mesh [V]

Notes:

  • Kirchhoff's Voltage Law states that the sum of the electrical potential differences (voltages) around any closed network is zero.
