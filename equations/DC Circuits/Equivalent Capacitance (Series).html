Equivalent Capacitance in Series

Equation:

$$\frac{1}{C_\text{eq}} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{1}{C_j}$$

Variables:

  • \(C_\text{eq}\): Equivalent capacitance of the series [F]
  • \(C_j\): Capacitance of the j-th capacitor in the series [F]
  • \(n\): Total number of capacitors in the series
This equation calculates the equivalent capacitance of capacitors connected in series. The reciprocal of the equivalent capacitance is the sum of the reciprocals of the individual capacitances.
