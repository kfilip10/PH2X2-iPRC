Capacitance (C)

Equation:

$$C = \kappa \epsilon_0 \frac{A}{d}$$

Variables:

  • \(C\): Capacitance [F]
  • \(\kappa\): Dielectric constant (dimensionless)
  • \(\epsilon_0\): Vacuum permittivity [F/m]
  • \(A\): Area of one of the plates [mÂ²]
  • \(d\): Distance between the plates [m]
This equation represents the capacitance of a parallel plate capacitor, which is dependent on the area of the plates, the distance between them, the dielectric constant of the material between the plates, and the vacuum permittivity.
