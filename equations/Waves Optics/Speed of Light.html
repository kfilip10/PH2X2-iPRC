Speed of Light in Vacuum

Equation:

$$c = \frac{E_0}{B_0}$$

Variables:

  • \(c\): Speed of light [m/s]
  • \(E_0\): Electric field strength [V/m]
  • \(B_0\): Magnetic field strength [T]
This equation relates the speed of light in a vacuum to the magnitudes of the electric and magnetic fields. It demonstrates that the speed of light is derived from the ratio of the electric field to the magnetic field in an electromagnetic wave.
