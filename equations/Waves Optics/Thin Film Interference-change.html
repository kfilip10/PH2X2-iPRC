Thin Film Interference (Change)

Equation:

$$2t = \left(m + \frac{1}{2}\right)\frac{\lambda}{n_0}$$

Variables:

  • \(t\): Thickness of the film [m]
  • \(m\): Order of interference (integer)
  • \(\lambda\): Wavelength of light in vacuum [m]
  • \(n_2\): Index of refraction of the film
This equation is used in thin film interference. It calculates the thickness of the film required for a specific order of interference, given the wavelength of light in a vacuum and the refractive index of the film.
