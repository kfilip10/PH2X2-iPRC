Lens/Mirror Equation

Equation:

$$\frac{1}{d_o} + \frac{1}{d_i} = \frac{1}{f}$$

Variables:

  • \(d_o\): Object distance [m]
  • \(d_i\): Image distance [m]
  • \(f\): Focal length [m]
This equation relates the object distance, image distance, and the focal length of a lens or mirror. It is fundamental in optics for analyzing image formation.
