Critical Angle (\( \theta_c \))

Equation:

$$\theta_c = \sin^{-1}\left(\frac{n_2}{n_1}\right)$$

Variables:

  • \(\theta_c\): Critical angle [degrees]
  • \(n_2\): Index of refraction of the second medium
  • \(n_1\): Index of refraction of the first medium
This equation calculates the critical angle for total internal reflection between two media. It occurs when light attempts to pass from a medium with higher refractive index \(n_1\) to one with lower refractive index \(n_2\).
