Wavelength in a Medium

Equation:

$$\lambda_n = \frac{\lambda}{n}$$

Variables:

  • \(\lambda_n\): Wavelength in the medium [m]
  • \(\lambda\): Wavelength in a vacuum [m]
  • \(n\): Refractive index of the medium [unitless]
This equation calculates the wavelength of light in a medium with a given refractive index, compared to its wavelength in a vacuum.
