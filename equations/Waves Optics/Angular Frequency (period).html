Angular Frequency (\(\omega\))

Equation:

$$\omega = \frac{2\pi}{T}$$

Variables:

  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(T\): Period [s]
  • \(\pi\): Mathematical constant Pi (approximately 3.14159)
This equation describes the angular frequency of a periodic motion, which is the rate of rotation expressed in radians per second. It is the inverse of the period multiplied by \(2\pi\).
