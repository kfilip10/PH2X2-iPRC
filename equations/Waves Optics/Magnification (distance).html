Magnification

Equation:

$$m = -\frac{d_i}{d_o}$$

Variables:

  • \(m\): Magnification (dimensionless)
  • \(d_i\): Image distance [m]
  • \(d_o\): Object distance [m]
This equation describes the linear magnification of an image produced by a lens or mirror. A negative magnification indicates that the image is inverted relative to the object.
