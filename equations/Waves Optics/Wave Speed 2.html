Wave Speed

Equation:

$$v = \lambda f$$

Variables:

  • \(v\): Wave speed [m/s]
  • \(\lambda\): Wavelength [m]
  • \(f\): Frequency [Hz]
This equation relates the speed of a wave to its wavelength and frequency. It is fundamental in the study of wave phenomena and applies to various types of waves including sound, light, and water waves.
