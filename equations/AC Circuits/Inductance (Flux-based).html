Inductance (L)

Equation:

$$L = \frac{N \Phi_B}{I}$$

Variables:

  • \(L\): Inductance [H]
  • \(N\): Number of turns of the coil
  • \(\Phi_B\): Magnetic flux [Wb]
  • \(I\): Current [A]
This equation defines inductance, representing the ratio of the magnetic flux through the coil to the current flowing through it. Inductance is an intrinsic property of electrical circuits, especially coils and solenoids.
