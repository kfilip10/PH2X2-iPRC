Capacitive Reactance (\(X_C\))

Equation:

$$X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C}$$

Variables:

  • \(X_C\): Capacitive reactance [Î©]
  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(C\): Capacitance [F]
This equation calculates the capacitive reactance, which is the opposition to the change of voltage across an element in an AC circuit. It is inversely proportional to both the angular frequency and the capacitance.
