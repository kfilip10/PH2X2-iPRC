Resonant Frequency of an LC Circuit

Equation:

$$\omega = \frac{1}{\sqrt{L C}}$$

Variables:

  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(L\): Inductance [H]
  • \(C\): Capacitance [F]
This equation calculates the resonant angular frequency of a simple LC circuit, where the inductance and capacitance determine the frequency of oscillation.
