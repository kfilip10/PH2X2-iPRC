Inductive Reactance (\(X_L\))

Equation:

$$X_L = \omega L$$

Variables:

  • \(X_L\): Inductive reactance [Î©]
  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(L\): Inductance [H]
This equation describes the opposition that an inductor presents to alternating current, due to its inductance. Inductive reactance increases with higher frequencies.
