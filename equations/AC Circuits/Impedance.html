Impedance in AC Circuits (Z)

Equation:

$$Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$$

Variables:

  • \(Z\): Impedance [立]
  • \(R\): Resistance [立]
  • \(X_L\): Inductive reactance [立]
  • \(X_C\): Capacitive reactance [立]
This equation calculates the impedance of a circuit that contains both capacitance and inductance. The impedance reflects the total opposition to current flow in an AC circuit, combining both resistive and reactive components.
