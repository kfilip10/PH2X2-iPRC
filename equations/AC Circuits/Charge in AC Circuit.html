Simple Harmonic Charge

Equation:

$$q(t) = q_0 \cos(\omega t + \phi)$$

Variables:

  • \(q(t)\): Charge as a function of time [C]
  • \(q_0\): Maximum charge amplitude [C]
  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(t\): Time [s]
  • \(\phi\): Phase angle [rad]
This equation represents the charge on a capacitor as a function of time in a simple harmonic oscillator circuit, such as an LC circuit, where the charge oscillates sinusoidally.
