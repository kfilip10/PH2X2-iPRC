Angular Frequency (\(\omega\))

Equation:

$$\omega = 2\pi f$$

Variables:

  • \(\omega\): Angular frequency [rad/s]
  • \(f\): Frequency [Hz]
  • \(\pi\): Mathematical constant, approximately 3.14159
This equation relates the angular frequency of a wave to its frequency. It is useful in describing oscillatory systems such as sound waves and electromagnetic waves.
