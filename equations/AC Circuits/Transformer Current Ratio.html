Transformer Turns Ratio

Equation:

$$\frac{I_s}{I_p} = \frac{N_p}{N_s}$$

Variables:

  • \(I_s\): Secondary current [A]
  • \(I_p\): Primary current [A]
  • \(N_p\): Number of turns in the primary coil
  • \(N_s\): Number of turns in the secondary coil
This equation represents the turns ratio of a transformer, indicating the relationship between the currents and turns of the primary and secondary coils. It demonstrates that the current ratio is inversely proportional to the turn ratio.
