Electric Potential Energy Stored in an Inductor

Equation:

$$U_L = \frac{L I^2}{2}$$

Variables:

  • \(U_L\): Electric Potential Energy stored in the inductor [J]
  • \(L\): Inductance [H]
  • \(I\): Current [A]
This equation represents the electric ootential energy stored in an inductor when a current flows through it. The energy is proportional to the inductance and the square of the current.
