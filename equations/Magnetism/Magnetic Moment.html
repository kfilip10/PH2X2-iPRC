Magnetic Dipole Moment

Equation:

$$\vec{\mu} = NIA\hat{n}$$

Variables:

  • \(\vec{\mu}\): Magnetic dipole moment [A·m²]
  • \(N\): Number of turns (dimensionless)
  • \(I\): Current [A]
  • \(A\): Area of the loop [m²]
  • \(\hat{n}\): Unit vector perpendicular to the loop (dimensionless)
This equation represents the magnetic dipole moment of a current-carrying loop, which is proportional to the product of the current, the number of turns in the coil, and the area of the coil.
