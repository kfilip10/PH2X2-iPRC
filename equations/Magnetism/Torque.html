Magnetic Torque

Equation:

$$\vec{\tau} = \vec{\mu} \times \vec{B}$$

Variables:

  • \(\vec{\tau}\): Torque [N·m]
  • \(\vec{\mu}\): Magnetic Dipole Moment [A·m²]
  • \(\vec{B}\): Magnetic field [T]
This equation describes the torque experienced by a magnetic dipole moment \(\vec{\mu}\) when placed in a magnetic field \(\vec{B}\). It is a result of the cross product between the magnetic moment and the magnetic field.
