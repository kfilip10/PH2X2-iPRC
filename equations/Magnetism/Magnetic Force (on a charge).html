Magnetic Force (Lorentz Force)

Equation:

$$\vec{F}_B = q\vec{v} \times \vec{B}$$

Variables:

  • \(\vec{F}_B\): Magnetic force [N]
  • \(q\): Electric charge [C]
  • \(\vec{v}\): Velocity of the charge [m/s]
  • \(\vec{B}\): Magnetic field [T]
This equation represents the magnetic force experienced by a charged particle moving through a magnetic field. The force is perpendicular to both the velocity of the charge and the magnetic field.
